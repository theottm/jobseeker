Our software helps our customers deliver your favorite products when you need them, from food and beverage to health and beauty going through retail, services, industry or transports. In order to better serve its customers, FuturMaster is going through a product transformation toward a SaaS platform. We are growing our R&D center and are looking for an experienced front-end dev to join the team while the project is still on its early phases. We propose a nice work environment with state of the art technologies and methodologies.


Your mission

  • You will join a small R&D team of developers located in France or in China
  • You will contribute to RESTful API definitions
  • you will contribute to source code writing mostly in Spring & Java
  • You will design and code with unit-tests and API tests in mind
  • you will contribute to programming best practices definitions
  • you will conduct code reviews
  • you will collaborate with Product Owners, developers and Architects
  • you will coach other developers

You

  • are enthusiast about web technologies
  • are looking to contribute on a software that is having an actual impact
  • have worked at least 4 years on large web applications and multiple micro services
  • can speak, read and write English
  • are accountable for your work
  • keep your knowledge base up to date

What we’d also love to find in you

  • you write tests first and despise bugs
  • you understand agile
  • you are comfortable with GIT
  • you know design patterns
  • you promote collaboration
  • you can speak French and/or Chinese depending on welcome office
  • you understand the following acronyms: UX, UI, OWASP, OOP, SOLID, CI, CD, TFS & lol

What you will find with us

  • No legacy, work on a strategic all fresh project with domain and technical experts
  • A comfortable renovated office close from transportations in Boulogne
  • An e-learning platform available to each team member
  • An agile environment
  • Some technical and functional expertise to contribute to and learn from
  • Room for your ideas to brew
  • Collaborative workplace and nurturing organizational culture
  • Multi-cultural setup

With operations in more than 130 countries, Total is a top-tier international oil and gas company and a major player in low-carbon energies. We discover, produce, process, sell and market energy in a variety of forms, all the way to end-customers. Committed to better energy, our staff of 98,000 employees helps provide customers throughout the world with products and services that are safer, cleaner, more efficient, more innovative and accessible to as many people as possible. Our ambition is to become the responsible energy major.

In our Saclay based research entity, we apply innovative, state-of-the-art technologies and skills to develop enabling and breakthrough technologies, technical tools and quantitative techniques and want to be at the leading edge of strategically-important research areas. Our activities will be accelerated through proprietary research, joint-industry projects, government and university-sponsored programs, and by leveraging alliances with corporate partners and other research centers.

Our strategic goals are fueled by research in the following exciting areas, where data science and Artificial Intelligence will be crucial and strategic: 

Earth Imaging, remote sensing 

Image analysis, 

Industrial operations optimization (incl. predictive maintenance, early fault detection, etc…, 

nlp (natural language processing) and Information Extraction, 

Energy management systems, smart grids, smart cities, 

B2C and B2B, contextual information 

Renewables energies 

forecasting 

Inverse problems 

New materials discovery, process modelling 

CO2 capture and storage

We are looking for an experienced (at least 5 years after PhD) highly motivated and outstanding data science and Artificial Intelligence Research Scientist to expand our research efforts and to coordinate existing and new research projects, working closely with businesses.

This position will be included in our scientific computing, data science and Artificial Intelligence R&D program in Saclay. The incumbent will conduct, set up and coordinate (in close collaboration with the Program Managers and Scientific Advisors) cutting-edge research to design and develop machine-learning and deep learning algorithms for serving our multi-disciplinary and transverse operations in all of Total’s branches (Exploration & Production, Refining & Chemicals, Marketing & Services, Gas Renewables & Power).

Main Activities 

Develop and apply innovative algorithms and tools to improve or create new data science techniques. 

Propose new research projects and ideas. 

Coordinate research projects and partnerships. 

Contribute to the development of machine learning models, conduct tests and improve performance. 

Be an active research scientist and represent Total by publishing peer-reviewed papers in international top-tier journals and by participating in major international conferences. 

Apply sound project management skills to produce high-quality reporting.

Profil recherché

PhD (or MSc/ME with equivalent experience) in data science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics or a related discipline.

Strong skills and experience in designing machine learning and deep learning solutions to solve time series analysis problems: forecasting, anomaly detection…

 Some background in operational research and decision-making models. 

Programming experience with R, Scikit Learn, Keras, TensorFlow or another equivalent deep learning framework. 

Programming experience in python, C++ or Spark. 

Experience with version control systems: Git…tc 

Willingness to travel a few times a year, domestically and internationally.

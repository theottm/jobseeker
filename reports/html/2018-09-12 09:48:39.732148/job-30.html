As a Database Engineer in our team, you will be part of the data services department and focus on building up the PostgreSQL as a Service infrastructure. This includes building software to run the service at scale and will include building software around databases to make it fun to use.

WHERE YOUR EXPERTISE IS NEEDED
------------------------------

  • Work with us on providing PostgreSQL as a service to fellow Zalando engineers and others, leverage both Kubernetes and the AWS cloud for a great developer experience
  • Build software to operate a PostgreSQL platform efficiently and at large scale and collaborate cross-functionally with other teams on topics of data storage and PostgreSQL
  • Know your playing field and learn more about AWS, Kubernetes and Go as we move forward
  • Think big-picture and constantly evaluate our work and processes, and challenge us to move forward
WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
----------------------------

  • Sound knowledge of PostgreSQL: From understanding SQL to engine internals and deployments, solid software development skills in Python or Go are a plus
  • An understanding and interest in system and infrastructure engineering with a familiarity with other current data storage technologies: NoSQL databases and more
  • Experience and interest in working existing and emerging cloud technologies
  • A passion for staying on top of emerging data-storage technologies

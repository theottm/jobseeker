We are searching for our office in Berlin for a:

BI Report Developer (m/f/divers)

RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Consult with internal business users and external business units for the development of Business Intelligence reporting
  • Collect reporting requirements and translate this to various reporting solutions
  • Interpret data and analyse results using statistical techniques
  • Perform project estimation, build/implementation and handover

KEY REQUIREMENTS:

  • 3+ years’ experience in Business Intelligence reporting, proven working experience as a Business and/or Reporting Data Analyst
  • Expert in data visualization tools
  • Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages (SSRS, Power BI, Tableau), and databases (SQL Server)
  • Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analysing datasets (Excel, SPSS, SAS etc); Python or R Data skills are a plus
  • Expert in writing SQL queries and procedures
  • Familiar with dimensional modelling techniques
  • Familiar with product release management and has hands-on experience
  • Experience with Site Catalyst, Google Analytics, as well as market research experience are considered a plus
  • Business fluent in English, additional languages are a plus

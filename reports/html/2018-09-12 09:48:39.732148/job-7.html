Job number 7 ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Software Engineer - Infrastructure (m/f/d) ResearchGate GmbH ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The web was created by scientists and for scientists, to foster scientific collaboration and drive progress for a better world. Join our team to take the web back to its roots and achieve that original mission.

We’re a team of passionate optimists from around the world and from many different backgrounds. Together, we focus on building great products that change the way scientists communicate for the better.

We love what we do. We connect the world of science and make research open to all.

Objective of the role

You'll bridge our infrastructure and operations efforts to help us automate our existing processes and increase the overall efficiency of our platform. You're a skilled engineer that will help our site reliability team ensure that ResearchGate is available, reliable, and fast at all times, supporting the 15+ million scientists and researchers who connect on our platform every day.

Responsibilities

  • Enhance engineering productivity by improving tooling and monitoring
  • Monitor, manage and maintain the stability and performance of our infrastructure
  • Collaborate with our product engineers to deliver scalable solutions

Requirements

  • In-depth understanding of modern web technologies like TCP/IP, HTTP, load balancers, web servers, caching
  • Extensive experience in managing distributed systems and optimising its performance
  • Strong experience in operating relational and non-relational databases like Postgres and MongoDB
  • Understanding of modern web application infrastructure like source and dependency management, build pipelines, continuous integration, deployment, logging and monitoring
  • Coding and scripting abilities in Python and other languages like PHP or Java
  • Strong analytical and debugging skills, including the ability to rapidly assess, analyse, and resolve complex issues with little initial information or direction
  • Hands-on experience with Kubernetes, Docker and configuration management tools like Puppet or Chef is a plus
  • Experience with security-related topics is a plus
  • Very good command of English

Your profile

  • You love technology and have a passion for infrastructure
  • You find simple solutions to difficult problems
  • You excel at engineering maintainable, efficient, and scalable software
  • You are capable of technical deep-dives into code, networking, systems and storage
  • You enjoy working with great people in a fast-paced environment
  • You understand our mission and want to help achieve it
Environment

You'll be working in a fast-paced environment where code is written, tested and shipped continuously. Our engineering team is passionate about building maintainable, scalable web applications that are constantly optimized to meet the needs of our 15+ million users worldwide.

Join our highly-skilled international team in our company headquarters in the heart of Berlin. Work to change the world of science and have a good time while you’re at it: we offer free, healthy food and many fun events.

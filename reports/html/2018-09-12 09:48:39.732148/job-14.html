Job number 14 ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Senior Backend Developer YEAY GmbH ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

YEAY is on a mission to build closer, more collaborative and more rewarding relationships between brands and consumers. Our app provides teens with a social space to shoot and share videos featuring streetwear styles they love. The app makes the videos shoppable by adding affiliate links. Brands benefit from user-generated product recommendations: creators earn rewards from the value driven through their videos.

We’re already available in 22 languages with hundreds of thousands of downloads and millions of video views, making us one of the fastest growing startups in Berlin. We’re a team of 25 fun-loving and motivated folks and we’re a truly international mix from more than 13 countries.

We are also very excited to be part of a brand new blockchain project, the WOM Token, helping brands tap into peer-to-peer recommendations, while empowering people to earn money through their content. The token will first launch on the YEAY app, before being distributed on a global scale.

Looking for a strong node.js backend developer to help us support our quickly scaling platform. You’ll be joining a small team of dedicated engineers that are working on a growing set of both user and company-facing products being built in an exciting new proprietary end-to-end shopping platform. You will be a key player in a growing dev team and you’ll see your work making a difference. We promise.

What we expect from you:

  • Strong proficiency with server-side JavaScript (2-3 yrs)
  • Knowledge of Node.js and frameworks available for it, especially Express
  • Understanding the nature of asynchronous programming and its quirks and workarounds
  • Basic understanding of front-end technologies such as Angular, HTML, and CSS
  • User authentication and authorization between multiple systems, servers, and environments
  • Familiarity with NoSQL databases (However, a strong understanding of SQL methodology is always appreciated)
  • Understanding fundamental design principles behind a scalable application
  • Proficient understanding of code versioning tools and methods like git
  • Experience with client-server API concepts like authentication, ACL scoping, and versioning is helpful
  • Experience implementing automated testing platforms and unit tests is a plus
  • Experience with AWS, Heroku, or other devOp platforms would come in handy
  • Experience with mobile client-side development in iOS or Android is a big plus
  • Experience making mistakes and learning from them is always welcomed
  • OpenAPI
  • If you have knowledge of other languages, like Java, Scala, Python or Ruby, this would be a plus but it is not a requirement.

Tasks:

  • Supporting our mobile client teams with robust API functionality
  • Implementation of scalable, secure, and performant data services
  • Integration of search functionality, personalization, and other end-user facing services
  • Being a part of discussions about scalable architecture with the rest of the team
  • Enjoying and taking pride in the work you do

What we offer you:

  • A young and dynamic environment
  • A place to work in the center of Kreuzberg at the wonderful Landwehr canal.
  • A chance to develop as quick as you are willing to grow.
  • An international team
  • Friday beers

Job Type: Full-time

The Job

We're looking for an outstanding Senior Fullstack Software Engineer to help tell Seerene's story. You'd be responsible for working across many layers and stages. This position is a rare opportunity to work for a high-growth startup and a seasoned management team.

Your Day-to-Day Role

  • Build, own and run microservices in Azure and manage their lifecycles
  • Implement frontend features
  • Driving major architectural decisions as a part of a CoP
  • Driving agile practices
  • Work closely together with your team's Product Manager
  • Support team lead at project planning

Your Work Experience

  • A university degree in computer sciences
  • Rich experience in software engineering, testing, and quality assurance
  • Successful past roles as a software engineer on complex projects
  • Expertise in agile development methods and techniques
  • Knowledge of continuous integration tools and techniques
  • Experience with SaaS concepts
  • Demonstrated impact on mid and long-term architecture vision
  • You're comfortable working in:
  • Python or Java
  • Relational databases
  • NoSQL solutions
  • HTML5, CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Angular

Attributes You Possess

  • Put team goals before personal goals
  • Ensure buy-in for your major decisions
  • Have an eye for good UX
  • Be an appreciated point of contact for our product managers and UI/UX designers

Important Details

This full-time, permanent position is based in Germany (Potsdam). You must be fluent in English, as it's the operating language at all Seerene offices, worldwide. It's a bonus if you have solid German language skills.

This job opportunity includes off-the-job training opportunities at the Hasso-Plattner-Institut (HPI), Potsdam, as well as participation in research projects.

About Us

Have a look at www.seerene.com and get to know Seerene a little better. Seerene is a deep tech spin-off of Hasso Plattner Institute. After some years of intense research, Seerene was founded in 2015 and started to sell its SaaS platform in the same year. We are more than 80 employees worldwide located in Potsdam, Berlin, New York, San Francisco, London and Hong Kong. Here's a short description of the platform we've created:

While companies have management systems for finance, human resources, and sales, CIOs haven't traditionally had that same transparency. Seerene's code+people management platform sets a new standard for how global CIOs manage their codebases and engineering capacity. It lets them see across thousands of projects to understand effort, productivity, and quality. With fact-based KPIs they can accelerate time to market and increase their pace of innovation.

Seerene is backed by top-tier venture capital firms and has customers around the world.

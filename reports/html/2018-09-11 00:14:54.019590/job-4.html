Job number 4---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Senior Data Scientist (m/f) for our geospatial data marketplace venture BCG Digital Ventures ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
About the Venture

BCG Digital Ventures is building a new venture –codename Interstellar– together with one of the world’s largest aerospace companies. Interstellar is a state of the art geospatial data marketplace that, for the first time, brings together a large number of previously disparate data sources like high-resolution satellites, drones, IoT, and many more. Interstellar enables geospatial service providers to develop and offer industry specific solutions for their clients. Our corporate partner’s strategic and financial assets give us a significant launch pad as well as a lasting unfair advantage.

We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist (m/f) to join our motivated team in Berlin.

Tasks

  • Define and develop scalable data management and processing architecture
  • Identify relevant geospatial data sources, integrate them into the platform and ensure correlation with other data sources
  • Team with customers and other key content building organizations to help bring their data to life
  • Investigate emerging trends and techniques for modeling and processing of geo-spatial data.
  • Validate analytics algorithms from 3rd party service providers to ensure their functionality and accuracy
  • Develop machine learning based algorithms to process, analyze, and interpret geospatial data in a scalable way
  • Closely collaborate with the CTO in order to align work streams and achieve the set partner integration and functionality launch goals
  • Partner with various teams to develop solutions and workflows that meet customer needs

Requirements

  • 4+ years experience in software design and development, preferably in a web-service environment; excellent knowledge in scalable image processing, machine learning, artificial intelligence and data warehousing
  • Familiarity with geospatial COTs packages such as ArcGIS, ENVI, etc; as well as open source packages such as GDAL and QGIS
  • Experience managing and analyzing large, complex datasets in the geospatial industry
  • Machine learning techniques and algorithms such as k-NN, Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests, CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs as well as tools such as Tensorflow, Keras, Caffe etc
  • Programming languages such as Python, R and Java
  • Ability to design and implement production workflows for real time data updates
  • Knowledge of the latest theories, concepts, and statistical methods for classification and evaluation of geographic data, including uncertainty and confidence measures
  • Experience in professional software development teams
  • Understanding of triangulation, point mensuration, block adjustment, digital ortho map compilation, digital elevation data generation, and quality control
  • Familiarity with data security and risk management is a plus
  • Ability to monitor and debug a very large complex system of services
  • Familiarity with some of the following technologies is advantageous:
  • Relational and NoSQL databases
  • Big data processing frameworks such as Apache Spark and Hadoop
  • Fluency in English is a must, German is a plus

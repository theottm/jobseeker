21Backend Developer (w/m) Relocately GmbH

We are looking for a skilled Backend Developer to join our team and be responsible for the server-side of our web applications and automation of the default processes.

You will work closely with Product to ensure that our server-side can deliver the features required both for the internal and customer-facing applications and together with our Frontend Developer evolve the core of our REST API.

Your focus will be the evolution of our server-side logic and algorithms, the definition and maintenance of our APIs and databases, and ensure high performance and responsiveness to requests from the frontend.

You have a serious passion for building server-side applications and prefer continuous deployment environments. You have a background in data and algorithm design and exposure to multi-tiered, microservice based architectures (e.g. through Heroku buildpacks). Since your work will be supporting frontend technologies a basic understanding of the frontend is necessary.

What will you do?

  • Be an influential part of product development, share ideas and effectively communicate with the engineering team and end-users
  • Using Node.js, design, develop and maintain server-side applications using REST APIs to support frontend web apps
  • You are proficient with databases and will retrieve data from PostgreSQL and Salesforce
  • You have a strong understanding of computer science fundamentals and can implement algorithms like Bin Packing
  • You will distribute microservices built with Heroku buildpacks
  • Use a test-driven approach, and establish both unit and end-to-end test methodologies
  • Build reusable code and libraries for future use
  • Design and implementation of data storage solutions

Do you have?

  • Strong work experience as a Backend Developer, with hands-on experience with programming languages like server-side JavaScript (node), TypeScript, Python
  • In-depth understanding of the entire web development process (design, development and deployment) and basic familiarity with frontend languages
  • Deep understanding of common data structures and algorithms, including performance analysis (Big O notation)
  • Very familiar with unit and regression testing, with knowledge of widely-used frameworks e.g. Karma/Mocha (JavaScript)
  • Knowledge of build and deployment tools, and ability to set up automated testing and deployment of code
  • Knowledge of user authentication and authorization between multiple systems, servers, and environments
  • Ability to build database schemas that represent and support business processes, and integration of multiple data sources and external services into one system, preferably in PostgreSQL
  • Proficient understanding of code versioning tools, such as Git
  • Any exposure to Salesforce or experience with Java a serious plus

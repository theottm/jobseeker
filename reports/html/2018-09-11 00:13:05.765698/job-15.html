Developer (m/w), english

scondoo gives shoppers the opportunity to earn cashback rewards for specific products by scanning their supermarket receipts – no matter which store the products were bought in. This makes scondoo the first retailer independent mobile cashback program in Germany. Through its service and with its UK based peer CheckoutSmart scondoo provides consumer brands with the opportunity to establish a direct link to their shoppers through the mobile channel. Thus creating a unique and effective direct marketing instrument for brands in whole Europe.
